Project Overview

Many people find it frustrating planning a trip on a budget, whether the trip be for business or pleasure. Individuals would like to experience the best their destinations have to offer, but a lot of times they can’t decide what will deliver the most bang for their buck. Many people use a lot of different tools to research their trips prior to embarking on their trips.

Research Plan

This study is looking to discover how people plan their travel on a budget. Specifically, this study will uncover how individuals research, save, and book their travel plans, and it will focus on how they feel about that process. It’s also important to understand the tools they use to plan their trips, and the frustrations and gains they experience from those tools.

The Problem

After conducting my research I found that many people struggle juggling multiple apps/websites when having to book transportation, a place to stay, and activites to do. Many sites are too overwhelming and it's hard to track your budget or split costs with a group having to deal with multiple applications.

Final Takeaways

With this study, I learned that there are multiple areas where the travel planning process could be simplified for users. With more research I think I could’ve nailed down a more niche problem to solve. If I was given more time I would’ve liked to further develop the budget and group sharing aspects of the app. My research led me to believe that a lot of travelers find it stressful staying under budget or planning activities with multiple groups. The general problem though, seemed to be that there are too many apps to choose from when booking different aspects of a trip.
